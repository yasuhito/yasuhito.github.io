QCQI/演習 2.6

 Apr 5, 2021

内積の定義より、

\begin{eqnarray} \left(\sum_i \lambda_i |w_i\rangle, |v\rangle\right) &=& \left(|v\rangle, \sum_i \lambda_i |w_i\rangle\right)^{\ast} \\\
&=& \left(\sum_i \lambda_i (|v\rangle, |w_i\rangle)\right)^{\ast} \\\
&=& \sum_i \lambda_i^{\ast} (|v\rangle, |w_i\rangle)^{\ast} \\\
&=& \sum_i \lambda_i^{\ast} (|w_i\rangle, |v\rangle) \end{eqnarray}
